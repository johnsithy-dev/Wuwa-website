About My WuWa Gallery

✦ I built this site to collect and show off Wuthering Waves characters and artwork. It’s a simple gallery where I can practice my Tailwind skills and share the designs I enjoy.

✦ This project is just for fun and learning. I’ll keep adding new characters, fixing the layout, and making it look cooler as I go. It’s my way of mixing WuWa with web dev.

✦ My gallery isn’t just for artwork it’s also a place to explore WuWa team builds and weapon setups. I want to highlight strategies, combos, and gear choices that make resonators shine in battle.

✦ In the future, I plan to expand this gallery with more WuWa content — detailed team build guides, weapon recommendations, and maybe even tier lists. I want this site to grow into a helpful resource for fans who enjoy experimenting with resonators and strategies.
